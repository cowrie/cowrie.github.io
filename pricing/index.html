FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

What is Cowrie's API-based SAAS platform?

Cowrie's API-based SAAS platform is a cloud-based software service that allows users to access and use a variety of API-powered tools and functionality.

How does Cowrie's billing work?

Cowrie offers a variety of billing options, including monthly and annual subscription plans, as well as pay-as-you-go pricing for certain services. Payment is typically made through a credit card or other secure online payment method.

Can I get a refund for my Cowrie subscription?

Cowrie offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for most of its subscription plans. If you are not satisfied with your subscription within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund. Refunds for subscriptions that have been active for longer than 30 days may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

How do I cancel my Cowrie subscription?

To cancel your Cowrie subscription, you can log in to your account and navigate to the subscription management page. From there, you should be able to cancel your subscription and stop future billing.

Can I try Cowrie's API platform for free?

Cowrie offers a free trial of its API platform for a limited time. During the trial period, you will have access to a limited set of features and functionality, but you will not be charged.

How do I access Cowrie's API documentation?

Cowrie's API documentation is available on the company's website and can be accessed by logging in to your account. The documentation provides detailed information on how to use the API, as well as code examples and other resources.

How do I contact support?

If you need help with Cowrie's API platform or have any other questions, you can contact the company's support team by submitting a support request through the website or by emailing support@Cowrie.com.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

Cowrie may offer discounts or promotions from time to time. To stay up-to-date on the latest deals and special offers, you can sign up for the company's newsletter or follow it on social media.

How does Cowrie compare to other similar services?

Cowrie's API platform is a highly reliable and feature-rich service that offers a wide range of tools and functionality. It is competitively priced and offers a variety of billing options to suit different needs and budgets.
